Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy and Terms of Use apply to all mobile applications (“Apps”) published by Alexander Hopkins Studio on the Apple App Store under the same Apple Developer account.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how information is handled when you use our apps.

2. Subscriptions and In-App Purchases
Our apps may offer auto-renewable subscriptions and/or other in-app purchases.

Information collected is used only to:

Some of our apps may request permission to track your activity across other companies’ apps and websites for purposes such as advertising or analytics, in accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.
Tracking will occur only if you explicitly allow it. You may choose “Ask App Not to Track,” and you can change your tracking preference at any time in your device settings.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.
Data may be shared only with trusted third-party service providers (such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services) strictly for app operation and improvement, and only in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention
We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality and comply with applicable requirements. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

8. Children’s Privacy
The age rating of each app is determined and displayed on the App Store.
